Madagascar: fantastic landscapes.

Different traditional living around twenty ethnic groups of Malgasy people.

Virgin coast of Indian Ocean and Mozambique Channel.

Many climate and natural zones like in a small continent.

Extremely beautiful mountains and valleys shaped by rice terraces and traditional villages.

Sudety Mountains: great land and history in heart of Europe.

Three nations in one large mountains.

Twenty five thousand kilometres square of mysterious land.

Great variety of local architecture created over centuries.

Oman: vast deserts end rocky mountains.

Ragged Hajar mountains full of deep canyons (wadies).

Green oasis with ancient aflaj irrigation systems.

Giant, colourful and pure dunes of Wahiba Sands.

Old culture with great hospitality and kindness.

Virgin and deserted coast of Arabic Sea with two quiet rocky archipelagos.



Mexico: great paradise of everything.

Endless variety of mountains and natural zones.

Remains of old civilizations.

World class cuisine.

Mariachi, music and dance.

Endless cost of Pacific Ocean and Caribbean Sea.



Croatia: delightful beauty of Mediterranean country.
